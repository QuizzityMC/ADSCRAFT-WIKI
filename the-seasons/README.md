# The Seasons!

A list of all the seasons on the ADSCRAFT Server. There were 11, Season 1 Starting in late 2022 and season 11 concluding in March 2025.&#x20;

Unless you're trying to do a historical deep dive, chances are you're likely to be looking for seasons 9, 10 and 11. These are the seasons which saw the birth of most of the current nations and the geopolitics that defines this server.
